根据op类型来进行Tuple的分类

EMPTY

LABEL
    left: string. label name.
    effect: Do nothing. Only for jump.

ASSIGN
    left: any type. value to assign.
    res: var. var to be assigned
    effect: res = left

NEG
    left: any type
    res: var.
    effect: res = -left

ADD, SUB, MUL, DIV
    left: any type.
    right: any type.
    res: var.
    effect: res = left X right
